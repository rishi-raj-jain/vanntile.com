When a logo is not enough

I'll use this place as a bin for my graphics. I showcase here wallpapers, illustrations, grafitti, designs and even some spells. ;) Hope you'll like them. And as always, if you see something you want, you message me by pressing the button below.

Layerspot Wallpaper Collection

This series is inspired by Material Design guidelines, created with the overuse of layers. With powerful color schemes I turned small details into real symbols. The themes illustrated are a splash of color, a sunset and a ravaged solar system. You can check the full article here

Colorchords Wallpaper Collection

This series tries to recollect feelings using various intense color schemes along a layout based on strings. Small decorations complete the environment inspired by psychedelic views of Nature itself.

Nature Molds Illustrations

This is a personal project where I tried to illustrate in a careless way another point of view on life itself. Molding nature is more difficult than it seems.

Console Designs

Molding technology into fantastic vector illustrations is the purpose of this project. I will continue to work on more when inspiration strikes. Technology is an extension of humans, and as Jason Silva says, we should imagine a natural human of these days with a phone in their hands.

Captioning Words

These are more or less lettering pieces. Buckshot is the name of a song talking about grafitti on Macklemore's album TUMIM. Giant is solely a contrasting grafitti inspired by a real grafitti.

This one is for all the whovians out there. A little piece of art from me to you. It's a poem written in circullar gallifreyan. You can decipher it whenever you like.

Sorcerer Supreme Magic

The spells of Doctor Strange, the Sorcerer Supreme of the Marvel Universe, are an amazingly complicated job for a wizard, as well as a comic book artist. This is my simplified version of them.
